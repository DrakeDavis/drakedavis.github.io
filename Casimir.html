Casimir

Age: 40

Race: Human

Casimir wants to die, badly. A former solder, Casimir made a blood-oath with his battalion that they each would die a valiant death in a hard-fought battle. Unfortunately, Casimir was the only survivor. He's spent the years post-war trying desperately to die at the hand of city guards or town drunks. This has taken a large psychological toll, and now Casimir is almost manically suicidal.

  • Alignment- True Neutral
  • Background: Soldier
  • Personality Traits: Aggressively suicidal, will fight anything.
  • Ideals: A drive to get himself killed
  • Bonds: Will have to work with Kyle to see which wars have been fought previously in this campaign, and who/what the enemies were.
  • Flaws: Stupidly aggressive, always wants to fight. Also, tries his damnedest to die.

Pros: A simple character that is open to any campaign hooks Cons: Might be a very short-lived character
