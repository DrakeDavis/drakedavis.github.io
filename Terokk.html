Terokk

Age: 9

Race: Aarakocra

Class: Ranger

Terrokk is a goddamn bird. What do birds hate? Probably everything - but the Player's Companion says they really hate everthing about Elemental Earth. Why is an Aarakocra on the material plane with a bunch of boring humans? Because he's a super elite bird assassin sent to kill elemental earth stuff.

  • Alignment- Neutral Good
  • Background: Super Cool Assassin
  • Personality Traits: Flys constantly, becomes enraged if he ever has to touch the ground. Really hates earth stuff.
  • Ideals: Wants to kill Earth stuff. Also is a bird.
  • Bonds: The player's guide is called "Elemental Evil" so I'm assuming there's some Earth elementals he can fuck up.
  • Flaws: Probably hates the party. Also racist against non-birds.

Pros: A simple character that is open to any campaign hooks
Cons: Might be a very short-lived character
