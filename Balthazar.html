Balthazar

Age: 90(?)

Race: Human(?)

Class: Sorcerer

Balthazar is a red-faced OLD man. He's senile and borderline suffering from dementia. He is a "Wild Magic" Sorcerer - due to him constantly misremembering and screwing up his spells. Balthazar doesn't know how old he is, or where he came from, his main drive for this campaign will be to find any family or friends that recognize him.

  • Alignment: Chaotic Neutral
  • Background: Hermit (or Sage?)
  • Personality Traits: Senile, and SUPER old
  • Flaws: Forgetful and easily confused

Pros: You get to hear my old-man voice
Cons: I have a terrible old-man voice
