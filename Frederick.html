Frederick Benedict of the Silver Hand

Age: 43

Race: Human

Class: Paladin

Rasied since birth by the Order of the Silver Hand, Frederick is a righteous zealot that has been commanded to destroy evil wheverever it lurks. Frederick serves the gods - all of them. The pantheon of Good-aligned gods grants Frederick strength to seek out evil beings and summarily execute them. Carrying a large hammer and an executioner's axe, Federick will always deal non-lethal damage - until a downed enemy has been determined to be evil aligned. He is unrelenting and unwilling to work with any evil forces, and will go to great lengths to kill anyone or anything he deems as evil.

  • Alignment- Lawful Good
  • Background: Acolyte
  • Personality Traits: Extremely religious, recites scripture and reads enemies their last rites.
  • Ideals: A drive to rid the world of all evil.
  • Bonds: As long as evil exists, Frederick will have a reason for this campaign.
  • Flaws: Stringent and severe, will never side with evil or commit any evil acts.
