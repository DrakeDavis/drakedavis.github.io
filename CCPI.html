What is this?

This program uses Google Trends to see which cryptocurrencies are trending in popularity.

The top 100 cryptocurrencies are retrieved from Coinmarketcap and then sorted by their increase in popularity.

This increase is defined as the average popularity score of the last three days when compared to the average popularity score of the last 30 days. (Indicating a recent increase in popularity.)
